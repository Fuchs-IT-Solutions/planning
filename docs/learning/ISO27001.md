# ISO 27001:2022 — Information Security Management

**ISO 27001** = Internationaler Standard für Informationssicherheits-Managementsysteme (ISMS).

## 📋 Überblick

- **Standard**: ISO/IEC 27001:2022 (neueste Version)
- **Zweck**: Etablierung, Implementierung und Verbesserung eines ISMS
- **Geltung**: Weltweit, branchenunabhängig
- **Zertifizierbar**: Ja, durch akkreditierte Auditor:innen

## 🎯 14 Kontrollbereiche (A.5–A.18)

1. **Organizational Controls** (A.5)
2. **People Controls** (A.6)
3. **Asset Management** (A.8)
4. **Access Control** (A.9)
5. **Cryptography** (A.10)
6. **Physical & Environmental Security** (A.11)
7. **Operations Security** (A.12)
8. **Communications Security** (A.13)
9. **Systems Acquisition, Development & Maintenance** (A.14)
10. **Supplier Relationships** (A.15)
11. **Information Security Incident Management** (A.16)
12. **Business Continuity Management** (A.17)
13. **Compliance** (A.18)
